About Annika

Your guide on the mat & beyond

Hi, I'm Annika! I've been practicing yoga for over 12 years and teaching for 8, guiding students in Jönköping and online through transformative movement practices.

My approach blends traditional yoga philosophy with modern body science — creating classes that are both deeply grounding and physically intelligent. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening an existing practice, you're welcome here.
